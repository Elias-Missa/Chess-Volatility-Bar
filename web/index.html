          Eval tells you who's winning. Volatility tells you how hard it is to play.

          Stockfish's eval ranks positions by best play. It can't see that one of two equally "equal" positions is a bone-dry handshake while the other is a single-move minefield. The volatility bar measures that difference: how punishing it is to choose the wrong move.
